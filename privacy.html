1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve the App, we collect the following types of information:

a) Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your Email Address, a Nickname you provide, and a unique User ID automatically generated by our authentication service (Firebase Authentication).
  • User Content: We collect and store the content you create, including prompts you save to your personal account ("Saved Prompts") and content you post publicly, such as shared prompts and comments in the community feature ("Community Content"). Community Content is visible to other users of the App.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Crash Logs and Diagnostics: To maintain and improve the stability of our App, we may automatically collect de-identified crash logs and diagnostic data.

c) Information We May Collect in the Future

  • Device and Advertising IDs: If we introduce advertisements in the future, our third-party ad partners (such as Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers or your advertising ID (e.g., ADID) to serve personalized ads.

d) Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Gemini API Key: Your personal Google Gemini API key is stored locally on your device using shared_preferences. It is never sent to or stored on our servers.
