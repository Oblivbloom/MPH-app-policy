Privacy Policy for Music Prompt Generator

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

Developed by Oblivbloom ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address through Firebase Authentication.
  • User Content: We store the prompt options you create and the prompts you save in your personal account on Firebase Firestore.
  • Gemini API Key: Your personal Gemini API key is stored locally on your device using shared_preferences. It is never sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • To manage your account and provide you with customer support.
  • To personalize your experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore) provided by Google to store your account information and user content securely. Your personal Gemini API key is stored only on your device and is not accessible by us.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can manage your saved prompts within the App. To delete your account, please contact us.
